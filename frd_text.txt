4️⃣ Developer Notes
Export GST Data → Excel (.xlsx / .csv)
Filters: Date Range, Party Name, GSTIN / State, Invoice Type (Sales / Purchase)
Auto Calculations:
Taxable = Qty × Rate
GST = Taxable × GST % / 100
TDS = Taxable × TDS % / 100
Total = Taxable + GST – TDS
Payment Received / Made auto-updates ledger
Excel formatting: Bold headers, numbers right-aligned, dates DD-MM-YYYY
Optional: Conditional formatting for pending invoices
OCR Integration: Auto-populate purchase invoice from uploaded PDFs / images
Outcome:
Fully GST / TDS compliant invoicing
Reduced manual entry through auto-upload
Accurate ledgers, reports, and party balances
Streamlined payment reconciliation and reporting
